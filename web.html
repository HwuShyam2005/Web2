Key Features

Drag Tyres

The Demon is the first production car to be fitted with street-legal drag-racing tyres, the Nitto NT05Rs.

Hemi V8

The Demon is equipped with an 840bhp and 770lb ft of torque 6.2-litre HEMI V8 engine, making it the most powerful production muscle car ever. It includes 25 major component upgrades over the Hellcat engine.
